Quiet Protect

Peace of mind for your family.

Quiet Protect sits between the incoming email stream and your relative's inbox. Every email is assessed for threat signals — impersonation, urgency tactics, suspicious payment requests, fake government bodies — and anything suspicious is caught before it arrives. Your relative's inbox stays clean and calm. They never know anything was blocked.

You, as the family manager, see a plain English summary of what was flagged and why. One tap to review, one tap to confirm. The scam is gone before it ever becomes a phone call.

Their email content stays completely private. You see that something was blocked and why... never the email itself. Their inbox is theirs.
