What you're looking at

These are click-through prototypes with hardcoded sample data. They show the design and interaction logic — not a live product. Nothing connects to a real inbox. No real emails are processed.

The scoring engine and API layer are built and calibrated, but not yet connected to the UI. That is the next stage of development.
